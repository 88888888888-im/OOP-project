Virtual pet Simulation

The Virtual Pet Simulator is a game where you take care of a pet by feeding it, playing with it, and keeping it happy.
 The pet will have needs, and you must take care of them to keep it healthy, and games, that users play to keep pet happy.


1. Pet (Main class)
name (String) - pet's name
age (int) - pet's age
hunger (int) - hunger level
energy (int)- energy level
happiness (int) - happiness level

Methods
eat() - will increase hunger level
sleep() - will increase energy level
play() - increases happiness level, decreases energy level
